Welcome to Tristan's website!

Biography

TRISTAN GLOVER (he/him) is a young performer with the Young People's Chorus and is a junior at LaGuardia High School of Music and Performing Arts. He performs classical, gospel, and musical theatre genres, while also composing and writing alternative hip-hop music. A core member of YPC at the World Choir Games, Tristan helped secure five gold medals and the world championship. He has sung solos from “Betelehemu” at David Geffen Hall, “I Feel Your Spirit”, and also rapped a solo from “Joyful, Joyful”. Most recently, Tristan is preparing for another tour with YPC in July and working on “Brahms Requiem” with the Senior Chorus at LaGuardia. When he's not singing or writing music, Tristan enjoys creating digital art.

What's Next?

This summer, Tristan will be going to Napa Valley in California, on tour with the Young People's Chorus of New York City. Together they will be performing at Festival Napa Valley's 20th Anniversary Summer Season. This will be Tristan's 4th tour with YPC.

Tristan is also currently working on music, writing lyrics and composing music to hopefully release Metamorphosis, his first album, around 2028.

Next semester, you can find him in LaG shows, starring LaGuardia's Senior Chorus and Gospel Choir.
